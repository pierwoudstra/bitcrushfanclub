bitcrush fanclub <3 is an art collective made of young multidisciplinary artists with a common interest in digital music and art.
‘fanclub’ means to us being fans of eachother; practicing fan culture symbolises our strive towards collectivity in art and life. bitcrush fanclub <3 consists of experimental pop artists who are mostly found in inclusive spaces. We center fluid identity and socially accessible art.
